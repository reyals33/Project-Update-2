About Open Air/Open Heart

Our Mission

Open Air/Open Heart aims to connect people through their most vital signs—creating meaningful bonds by sharing our biological rhythms in real time. We believe that technology can foster deeper human connections by allowing us to share our heartbeats and vital signs with loved ones, creating a new form of intimacy and understanding.

Our Vision

We envision a world where technology doesn't separate us but instead opens new channels for authentic connection. By sharing our heartbeats—the most fundamental rhythm of our lives—we can cultivate empathy, presence, and a deeper human bond.

Our Story

2024

Project Conception: The idea for Open Air/Open Heart was born during a Christmas vacation retreat. While visiting his mother (her grandmother) in New Jersey, the founder and his daughter shared a powerful moment that inspired a new way of connecting through our most natural rhythm—the heartbeat.

2025

Prototype Development: Our first prototype was built to securely and accurately share vital sign data between trusted connections.

Early 2025

Beta Testing: We shared the concept within close-knit community groups, gathering feedback and refining the experience. This feedback loop helped us shape a research-driven path toward a more thoughtful product design.

Present

Public Launch: Open Air/Open Heart officially opens to the world—starting our journey of connecting people through the pulse of life, one heartbeat at a time.
